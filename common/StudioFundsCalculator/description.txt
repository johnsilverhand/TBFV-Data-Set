a_n=1.1a_{n-1}+2n,a_n=320*1.1^n-20n-220

A ≤ 100
⟹ n = 0
∨
A > 100
⟹ (320 * 1.1^{n - 1} - 20(n - 1) - 220) < A
∧ (320 * 1.1^n - 20n - 220) ≥ A
